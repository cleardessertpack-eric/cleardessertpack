Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get free samples before placing a bulk order? ▼

Yes! We offer free stock samples to check our quality. You only need to cover the international shipping freight, which takes about 3-5 days via DHL/FedEx.

What is your MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)? ▼

For standard clear boxes in stock, the MOQ is only 1 carton (e.g., 1000 pcs depending on the size). For custom logo printing or bespoke packaging, the MOQ starts at 5,000 pcs.

How long is the production lead time? ▼

Usually, it takes 3-5 days for in-stock items. For customized orders, production takes about 10-15 working days after the design mockup is confirmed.

Are these boxes microwave or freezer safe? ▼

Our boxes are perfect for refrigerators and freezers (can withstand temperatures down to -20°C without cracking). However, plastic materials like PET/PS are NOT microwave safe.
