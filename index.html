FAQ

Questions wholesale buyers ask before ordering.

What is the MOQ for clear tiramisu boxes?

MOQ varies by model, material, colour and customisation. Standard clear stock designs generally have lower entry quantities than custom moulds or printed packaging. Send the required capacity and annual volume for a confirmed MOQ.

Can you make custom sizes or capacities?

Yes. We can assess custom dimensions, portion capacities, lid profiles and branding components. Tooling feasibility, development time and MOQ depend on the requested structure.

Can I receive samples in the US or UK?

Yes. Standard samples can be arranged for evaluation. Freight cost, sample lead time and available models are confirmed before dispatch.

What custom branding options are available?

Common options include printed paper sleeves, pressure-sensitive labels, inserts, colour-matched components and customised cartons. Direct printing feasibility depends on material and order volume.

What information is needed for a quotation?

Please provide the target box size or dessert portion, lid style, material preference, quantity, destination postcode, branding needs and preferred Incoterm where known.

How do you support US and UK importers?

We can provide export packing details, carton dimensions, product specifications and commercial documentation for order review. Compliance documents and test requirements should be agreed for the selected material and destination market.
