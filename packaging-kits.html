Yes! Our complete packaging kits are designed for maximum flexibility. For example, in our Premium Dessert Gift Kit, you can choose a 4-inch clear tiramisu box matched with a custom-length paper sleeve and a specific ribbon color, or order mixed kit configurations to cover several dessert sizes. We will provide a detailed SKU-ready packing list and ensure all accessories fit perfectly with the respective clear boxes.
To help new tiramisu brands and bakery startups minimize inventory costs, we support low-MOQ starter configurations. While our standard MOQ for fully customized printed paper sleeves and custom ribbons is 1,000 to 2,000 pieces, we offer a low-MOQ starter kit of just 500 sets using generic clear box sizes combined with custom logo stickers. This allows you to achieve professional branding at a lower entry cost before scaling up.
All items in our dessert packaging kits are shipped flat (including clear boxes, paper sleeves, paper bags, and flat-pack ribbons) to minimize shipping volume, save on freight costs, and prevent crushing or bending during sea or air transit. The clear boxes are protected with dual-sided protective films, and the entire kit is packed in heavy-duty, double-corrugated export cartons with waterproof plastic lining to shield against humidity and rough handling.
